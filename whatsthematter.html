WHAT’S THE MATTER?

A short film by Chris & Jack

Duration: 11:00 min
Genre: Quantum-Bureaucratic Comedy
Style: Monty Python meets Interstellar

Minute-by-Minute Script

0:00 – 1:00

JACK: I got it... but now I’m missing it.

CHRIS: You got what?

JACK: Yesterday.

CHRIS: You got... yesterday?

1:00 – 2:00

Jack pulls out a quantum notebook labeled “Time Re-entry” and stares at a glitching calendar.

JACK: Where is it today?

2:00 – 3:30

JACK: We thought matter was fixed. But maybe it’s just... scheduled.

CHRIS: Scheduled matter. That’s... terrifying.

3:30 – 4:30

Jack gives a TEDx-style presentation to mannequin audience. Diagrams float.

JACK: Matter = What you feel when you’re not thinking.

4:30 – 6:00

CHRIS: What if matter is a distraction? A prop in the sketch of existence.

Fan whispers: "You are both... technically correct."

6:00 – 7:30

They float in void. Jack stares into a folder labeled: “WHAT’S THE MATTER?”

JACK: If everything is matter... then what’s the matter with us?

CHRIS: We noticed.

7:30 – 9:00

Flashback: Jack receives empty folder. Inside: a note – “Are you sure this is even yours?”

9:00 – 10:00

CHRIS: Try holding this. It’s light. But it matters.

Jack resonates with a paperclip.

10:00 – 11:00

Jack walks into a world of cosmic signage: “YOU ARE HERE (Temporarily)”

JACK (VO): Maybe matter... is just meaning... waiting to collapse.

CHRIS: So... what’s the matter?

Fade out.

“You asked for it yesterday. You questioned it today. Tomorrow... it answers back.”
